02. The Biology

The "Sinsemilla" Effect

In the cultivation of high-grade hemp, the female plant is the star. When a female plant senses that no pollen is present, she pours her remaining energy into the production of trichomes—sticky glands designed to trap pollen from the wind.

The longer she waits, the more potent she becomes. The aroma intensifies. This state is known as Sinsemilla (without seed). It is a bloom fueled by frustration.
